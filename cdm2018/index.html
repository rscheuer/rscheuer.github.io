About Me

Heyo! Welcome to Ryan Scheuer's personal site. I am an information science student studying user centered design and digital media at the University of Pittsburgh. I'm really invested in design, typography, and creative coding. I'm currently the president of the first professional fraternity for technology oriented students - Kappa Theta Pi. I'm also the design chair of Design for America at Pitt. My favorite typeface is integral CF Bold and my favorite place to work is at Redhawk Coffee in Pittsburgh.
