New Time's Roamin'

Fonts in Use

Type design is a lot like making bricks. You take the time to make each one perfect, but eventually you hand it off to the real craftsman to make a big beautiful house. In this scenario, the bricks are fonts, craftsmen are graphic designers, and the big beautiful house is their big beautiful design.

My typefaces have been used by some world-class designers in lots of cool places. I'm eternally grateful to everyone who supports my journey into type design and uses my fonts in production!
