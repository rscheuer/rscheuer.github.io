What is MyH2Info?


MyH2Info is a research initiative aimed at providing crucial information and resources to the millions of Americans with contaminated drinking water. Around the country, over 5,300 cities and towns are in violation of the Clean Water Act’s prohibition of lead and copper pipes in public water systems. This poses a serious risk to millions of American children, who can experience learning disabilities, lowered IQ, anemia, and a host of other serious health problems when exposed to lead at a young age.

To address this problem, we are developing the most comprehensive database of water quality and safety information and incorporating it into an interactive map. This will let you, your neighbors, and eventually people around the country to know what your risk of water contamination is.

But that’s not all! We will also provide affordable water testing and filters so that you can ensure that  you and your family are drinking clean, safe water.
