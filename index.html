H2Info

Link

H2Info is an all-in-one solution to America's water quality concerns. It is data company with a public-facing website where users can find their home's risk level using our interactive map. I was involved with getting this project off the ground, along with pitching it to investors in multiple competitions. This period of presentation required the creation of slideshows that featured these infographics, logos, and mockups.

This product is still in the development phase, and I am working with the CEO to finalize the front end aspects of the site as well as the databse and map implementation.
